Biography

I am Qiaolin Yu, an undergraduate student at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in China & the University of Liverpool in the UK. My research interest lies in database systems, including key-value stores, distributed storage systems, graph database systems and HTAP database systems.

Currently, I work closely with Zhichao Cao at Arizona State University. I have submitted a first-author paper and a third-author paper to ATC ‘23. I also have a lot of industrial experience. I am interning at PingCAP, working on the compute engine of TiDB and TiFlash. Before this, I interned in the Azure Machine Learning team of Microsoft, the ByteHouse team of ByteDance, and the graph database team of Kuaishou.
